Aesthetic phone wallpapers by Canva

Everybody loves a nice-looking phone screen. If you’ve got a creative spirit, you’ll love these aesthetic wallpaper designs from Canva. More so because of all our customization options. Browse through our high-quality, fully editable wallpaper templates and create your very own aesthetic phone background in minutes.

From elegant and minimalist designs to vibrant and colorful layouts, there’s a wide array of options to suit every taste. Find the best aesthetic phone wallpaper by using Canva’s smart filters and easily sort them by style, theme, topic, or even color scheme.

Made by professional graphic artists, Canva’s aesthetic phone wallpapers can be used as is. Download your favorite design as a crisp, high-quality PNG straight to your device. Or add your personal spin to it and customize a wallpaper template using Canva’s free, online editor.

To get started, click on your chosen wallpaper and go straight to the editing platform. Take advantage of our user-friendly interface, smart tools, and extensive resource library to create a one-of-a-kind wallpaper. Make simple tweaks to the fonts, colors, and other placeholder content found on the aesthetic phone wallpaper template. Or move things around to your liking.

Add your own images by dragging and dropping them onto the layout, or by navigating the Uploads tab. Level up the visuals of your phone wallpaper by choosing from thousands of aesthetic free images, vectors, and graphics in Canva’s searchable stock media library.

By creating a Canva account, all your work is automatically saved online so you never lose any progress. Plus, you can return to your design at any time and make edits whenever you like. Once you’re happy with your custom aesthetic wallpaper design for your phone, click or tap on Download to save your work to your device.

Set the mood with an aesthetic phone wallpaper of your own making. Design yours today with these beautiful templates from Canva.
